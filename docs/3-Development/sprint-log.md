# Sprint Log

## TLDR

Sprint planning, execution, and retrospectives for the Fantasy Football Companion App development process across 16 weeks and 4 phases.

## Sprint Structure

### Sprint Duration
- **Sprint Length**: 1 week per sprint
- **Total Sprints**: 16 sprints (4 phases Ã— 4 weeks)
- **Sprint Planning**: Monday morning planning sessions
- **Sprint Review**: Friday afternoon retrospectives

### Sprint Ceremonies
- **Sprint Planning**: Define sprint goals and tasks
- **Daily Standups**: Progress updates and blockers
- **Sprint Review**: Demo completed work
- **Sprint Retrospective**: Process improvement discussion
